# Ad Placement Plan — LaunchStatic (Post-Approval)

## Principles
- No ads above the first paragraph of articles
- No ads styled as buttons or download CTAs
- No ads adjacent to download or email signup buttons
- No "support us by clicking ads" language
- Maximum 3 ad units per long-form article

## Planned zones (after AdSense approval)
| Zone | Placement | Pages |
|------|-----------|-------|
| A | Below article intro, above first H2 | Guides, how-to |
| B | Mid-article after second H2 | Pillar guides only |
| C | Below FAQ, above CTA card | All articles |
| D | Footer banner | Site-wide (optional, low priority) |

## Pre-approval state
- Zero live AdSense scripts
- No empty ad placeholder divs that resemble broken UI
- Cookie banner discloses future ad cookies when CMP is added

## Invalid traffic prevention
- Do not click your own ads
- Do not ask users to click ads
- No paid traffic to bare landing pages without content
- Monitor AdSense policy center weekly
