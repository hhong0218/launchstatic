Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

LaunchStatic ("we," "us") operates launchstatic.dev. This policy describes what personal data we collect, why we use it, and your choices. We designed the site to collect as little data as possible.

Data we collect

Information you provide

  • Email address — when you email us or use the contact form (mailto flow)
  • Name — optional, via contact form
  • Message / inquiry content — support questions, custom build requests, waitlist signups for paid packs
  • Subject line — optional contact form field

We do not operate user accounts or payment processing on this site today. Future checkout for paid packs may collect billing data via a third-party processor (e.g., Gumroad, Lemon Squeezy) — their privacy policy will apply to that step.

Automatically collected log data

When you visit launchstatic.dev, our hosting provider (Cloudflare Pages) and edge network may process:

  • IP address (often truncated or anonymized at the edge)
  • Browser type, device type, operating system
  • Pages requested, referrer URL, timestamps
  • Security signals (bot scores, attack mitigation logs)

We do not maintain a separate server-side database of visitor profiles.

Cookies and similar technologies

See our Cookie Notice for detail. Summary:

  • Google Analytics 4: Loaded only after you accept our cookie banner. Used for aggregate traffic measurement on launchstatic.dev / launchstatic.pages.dev.
  • Cloudflare: May set strictly necessary cookies for security and delivery.
  • Local storage: Stores your analytics consent preference (ls-cookie-ok).
  • Future ads: If we enable Google AdSense, Google and partners may set additional advertising cookies. See below.

How we use your information

  • Respond to inquiries — answer template, deployment, or custom build questions
  • Improve the site — fix broken links, understand popular pages, refine content
  • Analytics — aggregate traffic measurement when enabled (no cross-site profiling intended)
  • Advertising — display ads and measure performance if AdSense or similar is enabled
  • Security & abuse prevention — protect the site from spam, attacks, and fraud
  • Legal compliance — respond to lawful requests where required

Google AdSense and third-party advertising

We may display ads through Google AdSense or comparable networks. Third-party vendors, including Google, may use cookies to serve ads based on your visits to this and other websites.

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and partners to serve ads from your visits.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising via Google Ads Settings.
  • Learn more: How Google uses data.

Where law requires consent before non-essential cookies, we will implement a consent mechanism before activating personalized ads in the EEA/UK.

Cookie controls and opt-out

  • Block or delete cookies in your browser settings
  • Use industry opt-out pages: NAI, EDAA
  • Read our Cookie Notice for step-by-step options

Retention

  • Email correspondence: up to 24 months unless a longer period is needed for active projects or legal obligations
  • Server/CDN logs: per Cloudflare retention defaults (typically short rolling windows)
  • Analytics: per vendor settings if/when enabled (often 12–26 months aggregate)

Sharing with third parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with:

  • Cloudflare — hosting and security
  • Email provider — when you send mail to hello@launchstatic.dev
  • Analytics vendor — if enabled (aggregate statistics)
  • Advertising partners — if AdSense is enabled
  • Payment processor — for future paid purchases (they act as merchant of record or processor)
  • Legal authorities — when required by valid law

International transfers

Data may be processed in the United States and other countries where our providers operate. We rely on provider safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses) where required for EEA/UK transfers.

Your rights (GDPR / UK GDPR / CCPA)

Depending on location, you may request access, correction, deletion, restriction, portability, or object to processing. California residents may have additional rights regarding sale/share of personal information — we do not sell personal information.

Contact hello@launchstatic.dev. We respond within applicable legal timeframes.

Children

Not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect children's data.

Changes

We will update the "Last updated" date when practices change. Material changes to advertising or cookies will be reflected here and in the Cookie Notice.
