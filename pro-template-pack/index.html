Polished layouts for niches the free trio does not cover — with structure ready for translation files and client handoff.

The Pro Template Pack extends LaunchStatic beyond the free SaaS, App, and Waitlist starters. Pricing and license

The Pro Template Pack is $49 one-time per purchase. That is not a subscription — you keep the files you download. License terms differ from the free MIT templates: you may use the pack in your own projects and client deliverables per the tier described on our License page, but you may not redistribute, resell, or publish the source files on template marketplaces or public GitHub repos. End products you build for users are allowed; sharing the raw HTML pack with competitors is not.

Who should buy

Buy the pack if you ship multiple landing pages per year and want consistent quality without rebuilding layout grids each time. Freelancers delivering client sites save billable hours. Founders who outgrew the free templates but do not want a $29/month site builder subscription get a predictable one-time cost. If you need only one page and the free templates suffice, save the $49 until you feel layout constraints — there is no artificial feature lock on the free tier.

Technical requirements

The pack ships as plain HTML/CSS/JS — the same stack as free templates. Open files in VS Code, Zed, or any editor. Preview locally with python -m http.server or your editor's live server. No Node version to pin, no package lockfile to audit. Custom fonts load from system stacks by default; swap in self-hosted or Google Fonts if your brand guide requires it. Images use responsive srcset placeholders you replace with WebP or AVIF exports from Figma. Deployment targets remain Cloudflare Pages, Netlify, or S3 — anywhere static files are served.
